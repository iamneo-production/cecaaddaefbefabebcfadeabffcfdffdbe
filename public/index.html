SOME OF MY FAV CARS

Toyota Supra

The Supra comes with the 3.0-liter, twin-turbocharged inline-six engine with 320 horsepower and 315 pound-feet of torque, paired with a six-speed manual transmission.

Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a 2 door, high performance rear engined car producing 641 hp.

Mazda RX7

The Mazda RX-7 is a front-engine, rear-wheel-drive, rotary engine-powered sports car.
